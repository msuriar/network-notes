State,Description
DOWN,Admin or operational down.
P2P,Up and connected to a point-to-point link. Hellos are multicast to ALLSPFROUTERS reserved address.
WAITING,NBMA/Broadcast networks only. Used for existing DR detection when an inteface comes up connected to a multi-access network.
DR,This router is the designated router for this multi-access network.
BDR,This router is the backup designated router for this multi-access network.
DROTHER,This router is not the DR or BDR for this multi-access network.
LOOP,This interface is looped, either in software or hardware.
